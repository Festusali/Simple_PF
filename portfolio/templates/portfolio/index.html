Features of Simple Portfolio

Free, Simple, Easy to Use Online Portfolio

After initial account setup (Registration), you get access to your personal account where you will;

  • Create Projects
    You will be able to add projects that you want you potential clients to see and be convinced to hire you. Each project has three image fields which you can use to back up your claims. A picture they say is worth more than a thousand words.

  • Create Experiences
    Have you worked with a Fortue 500 company or assisted, either independently or colaboratively in delivering that awesome project that you are so proud of? The experiences section is the best place to talk about those moments you can't stop bragging about. Do it with style and win the trust of potential clients/employers.

    The 21st Century Online carrer has little to worry about certificates. However, they're not totally useless. If you have been certified by that industry big brand, here is an opportunity to show the world what you've got. The simple truth is that your certificates joined with your projects, gives you more advantage. Hence, we have given you the coice to share and stand heads high.

    Without a means of communication, and keeping contacts, your journey into being a freelancer or independent contractor is largely limited. We know what it means to loose the contact of that special client. And we know how it feels when you loose a contract because you were not reachable at time when the contract is due to be signed.
    Therefore, we have provided you with a simple message box where your clients can leave urgent messages for you without giving any stress.
