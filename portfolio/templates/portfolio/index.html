About

Welcome To Simple Portfolio

The best free and easy to use online portfolio website. Simple Portfolio is here to satisfy the need of teeming freelancers and independent contractors who may be having litle difficulties setting up their own portfolio website.

You may be looking for a way to showcase yourself and your projects/talents online but lack what it takes to getting your own portfolio running on the internet, look no further, Simple Portfolio is here to the rescue.

To start, just register an account to start enjoying nummerous benefits we have set up waiting for you to tap into. No need to pay a dime, no wasting of time. You're just a minute away from getting your own personal environment where you can showcase your interesting projects to potential clients leveraging on eye catching design and simplicity of Simple Portfolio.

Features

Elegant Features Out Of The Box

After initial account setup (Registration), you get Free Lifetime Access to your personalized portfolio where you will;

  • Add Projects

    You will be able to add projects that you want you potential clients to see and be convinced to hire you. Each project has three image fields which you can use to back up your claims. A picture they say is worth more than a thousand words.

  • Add Experiences

    Have you worked with a Fortue 500 company or assisted, either independently or colaboratively in delivering that awesome project that you are so proud of?
    The experience section is the best place to talk about those moments you can't stop bragging about. Do it with style and win the trust of potential clients/employers.

  • Add Certificates

    Chances are that you have acquired certificate(s) while climbing your career path. In this section, you have the opportunity to add and showcase these certificates to (potential)clients in an elegant fashion. Certificates joined with your projects, gives you more advantage. Hence, we have given you the choice to share and stand shoulders higher than competitors.

  • Have Private Inbox

    We know what it means to loose the contact of that special client. And we know how it feels when you loose a contract because you were not reachable at a time when the contract is due to be signed.

    We therefore provide you simple and efficient Private Inbox where your clients can leave urgent messages for you.
    Note: All your messages are forwarded to your registered email address. We also keep a copy in your account for easy access within the site and for references.

  • Have Personalized Profile

    In your profile, you can set your bio, social network accounts, skills, target clients or field of specialty, a short call to action, and lots more.
    We have made it easy for you to show yourself, but even easier for potential clients/employers to locate you and quickly see how you can help them achieve their needs.
